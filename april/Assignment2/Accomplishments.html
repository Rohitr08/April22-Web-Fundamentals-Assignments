Mahendra Singh Dhoni Achievment and Awards

Sporting honors

  1. ICC ODI Player of the Year: 2008, 2009
  2. ICC World ODI XI: 2006, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014 (captain in 2009, 2011–2014)
  3. ICC Men's ODI team of the decade: 2011–2020 (captain and wicketkeeper)
  4. ICC Men's T20I team of the decade: 2011–2020 (captain and wicketkeeper)

National honours

  1. 2007–08: Major Dhyanchand Khel Ratna award, India's highest honor given for achievement in sports.
  2. 2009: Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award.
  3. 2018: Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award.
